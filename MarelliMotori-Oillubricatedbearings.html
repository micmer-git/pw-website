E-Motors & Electric Drives

Oil lubricated bearing support analysis on electric generators with mesh-less CFD methods

Nicola Pretto (MarelliMotori), D. Pesenti & M. Galbiati (EnginSoft) | MarelliMotori

Abstract

Marelli Motori, a designer and manufacturer of electrical motors and generators since 1891, tested Particleworks for oil lubricated bearing support analysis, enabling simulation of oil flow path inside machines and modeling large-scale equipment behaviour.
