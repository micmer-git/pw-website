What is Particleworks?

Particleworks is a CFD software for the simulation of liquid and multi-phase flows. The mesh-less solver and the intuitive interface make the simulation process simple and fast, even for complex geometries with moving parts, like transmissions, electric-motors or IC engines.

The Navier-Stokes solver is based on a deterministic Lagrangian method — Moving Particle Simulation — that allows to simulate fluid flow, heat transfer and granular flow.

Particleworks is used in different industrial sectors, from automotive and aerospace to consumer goods, from power generation to the Food & Beverage industry.

SPH vs MPS

Understanding the key differences between Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics and Moving Particle Simulation

Feature SPH MPS (Particleworks)
Method type Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics Moving Particle Simulation
Formulation Strong-form (kernel approximation) Weak-form (differential operators)
Compressibility Weakly compressible or incompressible Fully incompressible
Pressure stability Can exhibit noise Pressure Poisson equation — stable
Typical applications Astrophysics, ballistics, high-speed flows Lubrication, cooling, sloshing, heat transfer
Meshing required No No
