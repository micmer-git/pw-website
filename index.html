Technology

What is Particleworks?

A revolutionary meshfree CFD solver that transforms how engineers simulate complex fluid dynamics

Meshfree Simulation

No mesh generation needed. Import your CAD geometry and start simulating immediately. Complex moving parts, free surfaces and multi-phase flows are handled naturally.

MPS Method

The Navier-Stokes solver is based on Moving Particle Simulation, a deterministic Lagrangian method for fluid flow, heat transfer and granular interaction.

Cross-Industry

Used across automotive, aerospace, consumer goods, power generation and food & beverage industries for lubrication, cooling, sloshing and mixing applications.

Methodology

SPH vs MPS

Understanding the key differences between Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics and Moving Particle Simulation

Feature SPH MPS (Particleworks)
Method type Smoothed Particle Hydrodynamics Moving Particle Simulation Deterministic
Formulation Strong-form (kernel approximation) Weak-form (differential operators)
Compressibility Weakly compressible or incompressible Fully incompressible Stable
Pressure stability Can exhibit noise Pressure Poisson equation — highly stable
Typical applications Astrophysics, ballistics, high-speed flows Lubrication, cooling, sloshing, heat transfer
Meshing required No No Zero mesh
Setup time Hours to days Minutes to hours Fastest

  • Granular flow optimization
