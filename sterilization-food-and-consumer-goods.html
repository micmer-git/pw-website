Industry Application

Sterilization, Food & Consumer Goods

Optimize cleaning processes, sterilization coverage, and packaging operations with precise fluid simulation.

Managing sterilization processes for food and consumer goods requires precise control of thermal or chemical inactivation. Air removal, deep penetration of sterilizing agents while maintaining product integrity.

Particleworks enables engineers to simulate the complex fluid dynamics of cleaning jets in dishwashers, the filling of bottles and pouches at production speed, and the sterilization coverage of complex geometries. The meshfree approach naturally handles the splashing, thin films, and multiphase interactions that are central to these consumer and food industry applications.

Key Capabilities

Cleaning jets directionality (dishwashers)
Non-Newtonian residue scraping
Sterilized surface visualization
Bottle/pouch filling speed
Packaging deformation
Multi-phase food mixtures
