Industry Application

Mixing & Separation

Design efficient mixers, impellers, and separators with accurate non-Newtonian and multiphase flow simulation.

The design of mixers, impellers, and separators involves selection of the right type as they physically do the mixing. Managing non-Newtonian viscosity and multiphase interactions are key challenges.

Particleworks' particle-based approach excels at capturing the complex free-surface flows, vortex formation, and material deformation that occur in industrial mixing processes. Engineers can evaluate impeller geometries, optimize screw designs, and predict power consumption with confidence -- even for highly viscous or granular materials that defeat traditional CFD approaches.

Non-Newtonian mixing
Multiphase mixing with granular materials
