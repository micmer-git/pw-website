Industry Application

Vehicle Management

Simulate water wading, aquaplaning, and environmental interactions to ensure vehicle safety in extreme conditions.

Simulation for vehicle management dealing with environmental interactions -- water intake on flooded roads, wading contamination with mud and snow, aquaplaning prevention. These are critical safety scenarios that must be validated before physical prototyping.

Particleworks uniquely captures the violent, chaotic fluid behavior that vehicles encounter in real-world conditions. From deep water crossings that risk engine flooding, to high-speed aquaplaning where tyre tread design determines grip, to impact forces on battery enclosures from road debris -- the meshfree particle method handles these extreme free-surface flows with exceptional fidelity.
