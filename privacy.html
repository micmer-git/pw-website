3. Cookies

Cookies are small text files that visited websites send to users' terminals, where they are stored and then transmitted to the same websites on subsequent visits. "Third party" cookies, on the other hand, are set by a different website from the one being visited by the user. This is because each website may contain elements (images, maps, sounds, specific links to web pages of other domains, etc..) that reside on servers that are different to that of the website being visited.

Cookies are used for different purposes: performing computer authentication, session monitoring, recording information about specific configurations of users accessing the server, language and other browsing preferences, etc.
